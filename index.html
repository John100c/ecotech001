About EcoTech UK Ltd

Leading the UK biomass industry with sustainable wood pellet manufacturing solutions

EcoTech UK Ltd is an industrial manufacturing company specializing in producing high-quality wood pellets from 100% recycled wood waste. Our state-of-the-art facilities process post-industrial and post-consumer wood materials that would otherwise end up in landfills.

By transforming this waste into efficient, clean-burning fuel, we're helping reduce carbon emissions and promoting a circular economy. Our pellets are certified sustainable and provide an eco-friendly alternative to fossil fuels for commercial and industrial applications.

Industrial Operations

Our Manufacturing Process

Advanced industrial processes transforming waste wood into premium pellets

Collection

We collect clean wood waste from construction sites, manufacturers, and recycling centers.

Sorting & Cleaning

Wood is carefully sorted to remove contaminants and prepared for processing.

Grinding

Wood is reduced to consistent-sized particles using industrial grinders.

Drying

Moisture content is reduced to optimal levels for pellet production.

Pelletizing

Wood particles are compressed under high pressure to form dense pellets.

Packaging

Pellets are cooled and packaged for distribution to customers.

Sustainability Commitment

Building a sustainable future through responsible manufacturing practices

At EcoTech UK Ltd, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's the foundation of everything we do. Our manufacturing process helps divert thousands of tons of wood waste from landfills each year, reducing methane emissions and conserving natural resources.

Our pellets are carbon neutral, as the carbon dioxide released during combustion is equivalent to what the trees absorbed during their growth cycle. By choosing EcoTech UK Ltd, you're supporting a circular economy and reducing dependence on fossil fuels.
